Our Daycare

Aracely's Day Care is a licensed childcare facility that provides a nurturing home environment and fosters the skills necessary for lifelong learning. Children learn through creative play, socialization and a developmentally appropriate curriculum. Aracely's Day Care is a home away from home where children feel safe and loved.

Our Kids

Aracely's Day Care serves children ages six weeks through preschool (4-5 years old). To better meet the needs of each individual child, there is a limited number of children accepted within each age group. Please call us at (801) 393-8550 for availability.
