Diet

Nutritious home made meals are made for all children. The meals consist of a balanced diet of vegetables, fruits, breads, milk and cereals. All meals and diets are approved via Infants Nutrition & Care Associates (I.N.C.A). Special diets can be accommodated for a child with allergies or requires special diet.
